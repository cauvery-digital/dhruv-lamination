ID card : identification card, also known as id card or i card, are official documents that are used to prove a person's identity. PVC ID card : A PVC ID card is a type of identification card that is made from polyvinyl chloride (PVC) material. PVC ID card are commonly used for various purposes, including employee identification, security guard, student identification, office i card, access control, and membership card.
